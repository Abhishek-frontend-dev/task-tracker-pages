What is an Attendance Management System?

An attendance management system is a digital tool that helps businesses track and manage employees' working hours, leaves, overtime, and absences. It integrates seamlessly with the other apps like WhatsApp, Google Sheets, etc. to generate reports. This will help in managing payroll, managing time, reducing errors, ensuring accurate employee data, and better resource management. With an employee attendance management system, businesses can improve employee accountability, reduce administrative costs, and enhance operational efficiency.
