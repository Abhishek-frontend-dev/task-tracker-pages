What is Geofencing Attendance Management?

Geofencing attendance management is a system that uses virtual boundaries, known as geofences, to monitor and log employee attendance. Employees clock in or out based on their physical presence within these predefined areas.

Key Features of Geofencing Attendance Systems

Virtual Boundaries

Define specific geographic areas for attendance tracking, ensuring employees clock in and out only from authorized locations effectively.

Automated Tracking

Automatically record employee entry and exit times without manual intervention, reducing errors and enhancing efficiency in attendance management.

Real-time Monitoring

Receive instant updates on attendance status and employee movements, enabling immediate action for discrepancies or time-sensitive operations.

Customizable Geofences

Create and modify geofences to match unique business requirements, accommodating diverse industries and flexible workplace arrangements seamlessly.

How Does Geofencing Time and Attendance Work?

Setup Virtual Geofences

Define specific zones like office premises, worksites, or client locations by setting up virtual boundaries using geofencing technology. This ensures only authorized areas are used for attendance marking.

App Attendance Marking

Employees log in and out effortlessly using the geo-attendance app, which automatically tracks their presence when they enter or leave the geofence zones.

Real-Time Alerts

Receive instant notifications about tardiness, missed check-ins, or attendance discrepancies, enabling quick resolution and maintaining accountability across all operational locations.

Data Capturing

Attendance data is captured seamlessly in real-time for thorough analysis. It helps monitor employee presence and ensures accurate reporting for compliance purposes.
