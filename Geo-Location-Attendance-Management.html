What is Geo-Attendance?

Geo-attendance leverages location-based technology to record employee attendance. Using a geo-attendance app, employees can clock in and out only when they are within a pre-defined location. This system eliminates discrepancies, ensuring accurate records.

Key Features of Geo-Location Attendance Management

Geo-Tagging for Real-Time Accuracy

Tracks employee location during clock in and clock-out. Ensures attendance is logged only at designated locations.

User-Friendly Geo-Attendance App

Simple interface for employees to mark their attendance. Instant notifications and updates for both employers and staff.

Comprehensive Attendance Reports

Generate detailed attendance reports. Get insights on employee punctuality and working hours.

Location-Based Compliance

Define geofenced areas to ensure attendance from authorized zones only. Prevent fraudulent entries with precise location validation.
